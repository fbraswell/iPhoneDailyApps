Will Rogers Museum

The Will Rogers Museum is located in Claremore, OK, just north of Tulsa. It's a great destination for a family vacation, and an invaluable resource for the serioius historian.

Rogers writings are a unique blend of humor and insight into the world in which he lived. What makes them even more important is that his quotes are timeless - as relevant today as they were then.

Dog Iron Ranch

For a journey back in time, visit the Will Rogers birthplace ranch. It is a short drive from the Museum and overlooks Lake Oolagah.

It is a working ranch and features picnic and camping facilities for visitors.

The Rogers house is beautifully restored and furnished, giving visitors a glimpse into what life was like 100 years ago in rural Oklahoma.

Among the many animals at the ranch is a rather fearsome guard donkey!
